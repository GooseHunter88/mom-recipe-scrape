    Perfect Hard Boiled Eggs

    1. Lay eggs in a saucepan and cover with water (at least 2" above eggs)
    2. Bring to a full boil over high heat
    3. Turn off heat, cover, and let stand 10-12 minutes
    4. Drain and run under cold water
    5. Peel under cool running water

    How to Cook Spaghetti Squash

    1. Preheat oven to 400°
    2. Slice medium squash (2-3 lbs) lengthwise, stem to tail
    3. Scoop out seeds and stringy bits
    4. Place cut-side down in roasting pan with water covering bottom
    5. Roast 30-45 minutes until fork easily pierces flesh
    6. Use fork to separate flesh into strands
    7. Serve with butter or olive oil
    Storage Tip
    Spaghetti squash can be refrigerated up to one week or frozen up to 3 months.
